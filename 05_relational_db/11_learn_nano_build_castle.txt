Nano is a program for editing files that runs in the terminal. You can open a file with
    nano filename
This will bring you to the nano editor, with the available commands on the bottom
The ^ in front of all the commands at the bottom means to press control and the letter to run the command
The M at the beginning of the other commands at the bottom stands for "meta". It's a key that doesn't exist on most keyboards. On Windows, it's ALT then the letter
